Shipping and Delivery

We understand that reliable and efficient shipping and delivery services are vital to the success of international trade. We are committed to ensuring that your products reach their destination promptly, securely, and in impeccable condition. Our Shipping & Delivery services are designed to provide you with a hassle-free experience, no matter where in the world you are.


At TIB Global Enterprise LLC we can deliver your goods directly to:


  • Your warehouse.
  • Any port worldwide.
  • Your shipping agent.

We can also you help to find the best shipping agent. Shipping cost is paid by buyer.


We will make every attempt to ship out your order as fast as possible. We organize fast dispatches and timely shipping and deliveries from our factory and warehouses across the globe, or directly from the manufacturers. Once your load is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with all the necessary information about your shipment. We ship worldwide. However, to prevent fraud we reserve the right at our sole discretion to refuse shipping an order without prior notice and explanation.


Transport for shipment.

  • Sea Container 20″, loading weight 24 MT.
  • Sea Container 40″, loading weight 28.4 MT

Rail/Road Transportation:

  • Transport carry out by cargo trucks, loading weight 20 metric ton (MT), TIR or CMR.
  • Wagon, loading weight 68 MT.

Our Commitment to You

  • Timely Deliveries: We prioritize punctuality. We work diligently to meet agreed-upon delivery timelines, ensuring that your products are in your hands when you expect them.
  • Global Reach:TIB Global Enterprise LLC extensive network of shipping partners allows us to offer delivery services to a vast array of international destinations. No matter where you are located, we can reach you.
  • Secure Packaging:We understand the importance of product safety. Our packaging experts ensure that your items are meticulously packaged to withstand the rigors of international shipping and arrive in pristine condition.
  • Tracking and Transparency:We provide you with real-time tracking information, allowing you to monitor the progress of your shipment every step of the way. You’ll always know where your products are.
  • Customs and DocumentationDealing with customs and documentation can be complex. Our experienced team will handle all necessary customs and documentation requirements, ensuring a smooth clearance process.

Sustainability in Shipping

TIB Global Enterprise LLC is committed to sustainable shipping practices. We actively seek eco-friendly shipping options, reduce waste, and choose carriers with environmentally responsible practices whenever possible. We believe in doing our part to minimize our impact on the planet.


Customer Support

Our customer support team is always available to assist you with any shipping or delivery-related inquiries. Whether you have questions about shipping methods, tracking, or delivery times, we are here to provide the information and support you need.
